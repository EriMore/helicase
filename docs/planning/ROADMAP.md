# Roadmap

## Now: Atlas foundation

Establish the scene architecture, curated data layer, confidence visualization, GPT tool loop, and one unforgettable design playback.

## Next: Research workspace

Support richer comparison, saved investigations, broader structure and annotation coverage, and configurable exploration without turning the product into an administrative dashboard.

## Later: Design lifecycle

Connect arbitrary target specifications, multi-objective design, reproducible jobs, experiment planning, and laboratory feedback. These require stronger provenance, permissions, compute orchestration, and scientific validation.

## Long-term thesis

Helicase can become an operating environment for molecular reasoning: a place where observations, hypotheses, simulations, generated candidates, and validated experiments remain connected.

## Roadmap discipline

Future capability must preserve the core experience: the user should be able to see what the system knows, what it predicts, what it proposes, and what action follows.
