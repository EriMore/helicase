# Helicase Experience Principles

## The experience thesis

Helicase is a scientific instrument disguised as a place. It should feel like entering the frontier of biology, not opening laboratory software.

## Principles

1. **Make the object present.** The protein is the interface; controls orbit it.
2. **Let discovery be earned.** A reveal follows a question, a selection, or a meaningful change in viewpoint.
3. **Turn evidence into atmosphere.** Confidence, provenance, and uncertainty should be felt before they are read.
4. **Use restraint as sophistication.** Near-black space, quiet chrome, and rare accents create authority.
5. **One continuous world.** Map, structure, uncertainty, and design are scales of one place.
6. **Explain through consequence.** GPT earns trust when its reasoning changes the scene.
7. **Make uncertainty beautiful, never vague.** Fog means unresolved prediction; it must remain legible as such.
8. **Protect the pause.** A moment of resolution needs time to land.
9. **No ornamental science fiction.** Every visual metaphor must map to a scientific or interaction concept.
10. **Precision is emotional.** Exact labels, careful timing, and honest qualifiers make wonder credible.

## Emotional arc

Wonder → orientation → trust → inquiry → uncertainty → creation → reflection.
