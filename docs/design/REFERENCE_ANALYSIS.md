# Reference Analysis

## Research note

The references were inspected through their publicly accessible pages and indexed content on 18 July 2026. Live DOM, WebGL timing, and device-specific behavior were not fully inspectable for every page from the research environment; observations below distinguish visible structure from inference. No visual assets or copy are to be copied.

## Orano Innovation

**Exceptional:** positions innovation as a journey through future possibilities, industrial transitions, and open collaboration. The surrounding Orano material emphasizes foresight, design, modeling, and visualizing ideas before execution.

**Use for Helicase:** give the experience a clear sense of progression and consequence. Let each transition move the user toward a question, not merely another section.

**Do not copy:** corporate innovation language, category navigation, or a linear marketing-story structure.

**Limitation:** the supplied slider URL returned no extractable page content, so interaction-specific claims are intentionally limited.

## Montfort Group

**Exceptional:** uses scroll/swipe language, large typographic spacing, and a structured sequence to turn several business divisions into a navigable story. The site makes global scale and organizational relationships legible.

**Use for Helicase:** use scale and sequential reveal to make the protein universe feel navigable. Treat clusters as meaningful neighborhoods rather than a random point cloud.

**Do not copy:** corporate division cards, generic “swipe down” prompts, or commodity-brand language.

## Lusion About

**Exceptional:** makes multidisciplinary craft explicit—strategy, creative, technology, motion, 3D, and production—while using scroll, numbered chapters, and an immersive editorial rhythm. The experience presents technical craft as part of the brand.

**Use for Helicase:** make the system’s scientific and technical process visible in the experience. Give the design reveal a clear chapter rhythm and let the object remain the hero.

**Do not copy:** agency portfolio conventions, award-count spectacle, or decorative 3D without a domain consequence.

## OSE Engineering

**Exceptional:** combines a strong engineering proposition with a clear activity taxonomy, a structured methodology, and controlled motion/media controls. It makes rigor and acceleration coexist.

**Use for Helicase:** pair cinematic opening moments with persistent methodological cues: source, pipeline stage, confidence, and next action.

**Do not copy:** industrial service navigation, dense sector menus, or promotional data blocks.

## Glyphic Biotechnologies

**Exceptional:** turns a technical protein-sequencing process into a sequential “turn the page” narrative: preparation, expansion, sequencing. The mechanism itself is the story, and repeated restrained typography reinforces precision.

**Use for Helicase:** model the design reveal as a legible process with named stages. Show what the pipeline is doing before showing a final score.

**Do not copy:** the specific brand treatment, spaced wordmark, or claim language.

## AIR Business Center

**Exceptional:** establishes monumentality through vertical rhythm, architectural scale, restrained material language, and progressive disclosure. Its information hierarchy lets a large spatial proposition feel coherent rather than crowded.

**Use for Helicase:** communicate scale through relative motion and quiet landmarks. Region names, protein counts, and the current camera depth should orient without flattening the universe into navigation chrome.

**Do not copy:** luxury-property visual codes, sales interactions, or monumental scale without informational meaning.

**Limitation:** indexed structure and media were inspectable, but the live WebGL timing and device-specific transitions were not fully available in the research environment.

## Wembi

**Exceptional:** frames a complex digital-twin system through numbered chapters and a direct what/how/why progression. It repeatedly connects representation to real-time state, prediction, and control.

**Use for Helicase:** make every scene transformation answer what changed, why it changed, and what evidence supports it. Typed AI commands should visibly operate the same scientific scene as direct manipulation.

**Do not copy:** dashboard framing, generic real-time telemetry language, or numbered sections as permanent application navigation.

**Limitation:** page structure and content were inspectable; detailed transition easing and responsive behavior were not fully observable.

## Cross-reference patterns

### Shared strengths

- Scroll or swipe acts as a narrative gesture.
- Large type creates confidence before detail arrives.
- Technical work is shown as a sequence, not a static claim.
- Strong chapters create rhythm and memory.
- Precision is communicated through restraint and repetition.

### Missed opportunities for Helicase

- Most references are authored narratives, not user-directed scientific instruments.
- They do not make model uncertainty physically visible.
- They do not let a user ask a question and watch reasoning alter a spatial object.
- They do not combine exploration, evidence, and generative hypothesis in one continuous world.

## Helicase divergence

Helicase must replace scroll-as-content with camera-as-understanding. The user does not consume a prewritten page; they enter a coordinate system. The decisive interaction is not “next section” but “show me why this matters,” followed by a visible, reversible scene change.

## Memorability candidates

1. A star’s label resolves only as the camera reaches its molecular structure.
2. “Where should I not trust this model?” turns a solved-looking molecule into a crisp core and living fog.
3. “Design a small binder for this site” causes a named pipeline to unfold from noise to a docked candidate.
4. Pulling back reveals that the current molecule is one address in a much larger, navigable space.

## Friction to avoid

- Forcing users through onboarding before the first image.
- Hiding the map behind menus.
- Making the copilot explain actions without taking them.
- Making the user decode scientific colors without a legend.
- Allowing a loading state to look like an empty or broken scene.
